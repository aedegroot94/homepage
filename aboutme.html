About this website

I wrote this website as a fun programming challenge, and to build a portfolio to show off past and future code projects. I decided to include a music section because I'm a passionate amateur musician and I will never pass up a chance to talk about this part of my life.

About Me

My name is Anne. I was born in Germany in 1994. The year after I was born my parents moved to Eerbeek in the Netherlands, the home town of my mother and the town I would grow up in. Currently I live in Apeldoorn with my boyfriend Tom, our cat Poppy and our two (soon to be four!) rats, Luna and Leia.

I currently work as a cloud engineer at Universal IT in Arnhem, a city near my home town. I started off as a support engineer but I currently spend my days working on internal projects: I come up with and implement solutions to make life easier for my coworkers, or I do things that need to happen but no one has found the time for yet.

When I'm not working I spend a lot of time on my big passion: Music. I am an amateur horn player and I currently play in two fanfare orchestras. More about what I do and how this came to be can be found on the page I dedicated to this subject.
